I am a **Machine Learning Researcher** specializing in bridging the gap between fundamental science and real-world applications. With a **Ph.D. in Physics** and a background in **Computational Chemistry**, I focus on building scalable discovery systems through advanced **RAG architectures**, **AI Agents (LangGraph)**, and automated pipelines for high-dimensional scientific data.
